Biography

I am Jeff Wang (王啸峰), currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University. I received my Ph.D. from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in 2025. During my Ph.D., I published 20 papers in venues including CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICLR, and AAAI. My work DriveDreamer was selected as one of the Most Influential ECCV'24 Papers, and I was invited by NVIDIA for an oral presentation. DriveDreamer has been deployed at most leading OEMs across China.

I also serve as an Algorithm Partner at GigaAI, and work closely with our Chief Scientist Zheng Zhu. I lead frontier research and production deployment of world models. Our representative projects include DriveDreamer, GigaBrain, and GigaWorld. We are actively hiring full-time members and interns. Our members include recipients of prestigious programs like Huawei Genius Young Scholars (华为天才少年) and Alibaba Star (阿里星). Feel free to reach out at this email.
